What is Fleuris?

Fleuris is a unique marketplace designed especially for girls and women in Egypt who love fashion, beauty, and accessories that stand out. We bring together local designers and micro-brands in one beautiful place so you can discover and shop styles you won’t find anywhere else.

Why Choose Fleuris?

  • Exclusive Collections: Carefully curated products from local designers and emerging brands.
  • Cash on Delivery: Shop worry-free with Egypt-friendly payment options.
  • Community & Rewards: Connect with sellers, earn badges, and get special discounts.
  • Style Help: Use our AI Stylist to find outfits that suit you perfectly (coming soon!).
  • Easy and Safe: Buyer and seller protection with a simple, mobile-friendly experience.

Who is Fleuris For?

Buyers: If you love unique fashion and want a trusted, stylish place to shop locally.

Sellers: If you’re a designer or small brand wanting to reach new customers with zero hassle.
